These movie lines are obtained from the Cornell Movie-Dialogs Corpus

Link: https://www.cs.cornell.edu/~cristian/Cornell_Movie-Dialogs_Corpus.html

For English EUC:
The data training used is the whole movie lines and conversations

For Indonesian EUC:
The data training used is the first 50.000 movie lines from the original file, translated using Google Translate (translate.google.com)

For Mixed EUC:
The data training used is the combination of 50.000 movie lines translated using Google Translate and 50.000 movie lines from the original file
